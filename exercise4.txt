exercise 4.1
============

Assumption: fmap f m = pure f <*> m

1a:

fmap id v
{assumption}
pure id <*> v

1b:

fmap (f.g) m
{assumption}
pure (f.g) <*> m
{2c}
pure ((.) f) <*> pure g <*> m
{2c}
pure (.) <*> pure f <*> pure g <*> m
{2b}
pure f <*> (pure g <*> m)
{assumption}
fmap f (pure g <*> m)
{assumption}
fmap f (fmap g m)
{(.)}
(fmap f . fmap g) m
